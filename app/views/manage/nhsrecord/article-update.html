Important: Your NHS contact details and NHS login details Your NHS contact details and NHS login details

Your NHS login details are used to log in to your NHS account and other healthcare apps and websites.

Your contact details are the details that your GP and other NHS services use to contact you.

Updating your NHS record with your NHS login details

When you create an NHS login account, the phone number and email address you use are only stored on this account. These are your NHS login details.

However, if we find that the contact details on your NHS record are different to your NHS login details, we will ask if you want to update them.

For example, we will show you the phone number we have on your NHS login account and the phone number on your NHS record. You can then choose which one the NHS should use to contact you.

How to change your contact details on your NHS record

If you need to update the phone number or email address on your NHS record, you must first prove who you are.

You can update your contact details by logging into your NHS account or by using the NHS App.

If contact details are missing from your NHS record, or if any of your details are in an incorrect format, we will send you a text message or email to let you know.

Information:

Some NHS services, like your GP surgery, may continue to use your previous contact details. This is because there may be a delay before they check your updated NHS record.
