Contact NHS login support

Information:

The NHS login team can only provide technical support. We cannot help with medical problems.

Immediate action required: If you have a medical emergency:

  • call 999
  • find your nearest A&E
  • get urgent help for mental health

Non-urgent advice: If you have a non-urgent medical query:

  • go to 111.nhs.uk or call 111
  • speak to a pharmacist, GP or dentist
  • search the NHS website health A-Z for more advice about your condition
  • find information and support for your mental health on the NHS website
